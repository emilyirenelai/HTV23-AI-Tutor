Question: What is the inefficient way to use a database connection?
Answer: connecting and disconnecting multiple times every time the database is required.
Question: What is the benefit of the singleton pattern for database connections?
Answer: It involves setting up the database only once. Then, whichever methods require calling the database will leverage this connection.
Question: In addition to being fast, what are the benefits of efficient design?
Answer: it is also beneficial for the environment, as it saves energy and processing power.
