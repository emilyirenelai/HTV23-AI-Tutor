 Question: What is the main idea of this lesson?
Answer: Connecting to databases can be expensive in terms of time and resources, therefore, it is efficient to connect once and perform all the necessary functions dealing with the database.

Question: Why is it expensive to connect and disconnect from a database multiple times?
Answer: Because it requires spending time and company resources for each connection and disconnection.

Question: What is the Singleton Pattern?
Answer: The Singleton Pattern is a design pattern that restricts the instantiation of a class to one object. It is often used for database connections.

Question: Why is it beneficial for the environment to save energy and processing power?
Answer: Because the excessive connections and disconnections to the database are avoided. This, in turn, reduces the carbon footprint.
