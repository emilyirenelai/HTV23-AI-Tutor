 Question: What is the main idea of this passage?
Answer: Connecting to databases can be expensive in terms of time and resources, therefore, it is efficient to connect once and use the connection for all required methods.

Question: What is the inefficient way of using database connections?
Answer: Connecting and disconnecting multiple times whenever the database is needed.

Question: What is the benefit of following the singleton pattern in database connections?
Answer: It avoids excessive connections and disconnections, making it more efficient.

Question: What is the primary business concern with the singleton pattern?
Answer: It is fast and saves the company time and money.

Question: How is the singleton pattern beneficial for the environment?
Answer: It saves energy and processing power by avoiding unnecessary connections and disconnections.
